Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OCDATA (“we,” “us,” “our”) handles information when you use the OCDATA - MC application (“App”).

1. Information We Access

With your permission, the App reads QuickBooks Online accounting data needed for analysis, reporting, and advisory insights.

2. How We Use Information

We use accessed data to generate financial analysis and summaries. The App is designed for read-only access and does not write back to your QuickBooks records.

3. Data Minimization and Redaction

Before analysis workflows, sensitive fields are reduced or redacted where feasible to minimize exposure of personal or confidential details.

4. Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted at rest. We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with service providers necessary to operate the App (for example, Intuit for QuickBooks connectivity and AI processing providers for analysis output).

6. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Your Choices

You can revoke App access from your Intuit account at any time. You may also contact us to request deletion of locally stored app artifacts, where applicable.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy by posting a revised version at this URL. Continued use of the App after updates indicates acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: support@ocdata.us
